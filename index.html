• Small Business Recovery Begins With INSIGHT® .

    The outbreak of the coronavirus pandemic has left businesses hurting or
    at risk of closure due to COVID-induced revenue loss. Indianapolis has a remarkable
    restaurant scene as well as tons of local stores serving the community.
    INSIGHT is a solution aimed at helping these small businesses survive and adapt
    during these unprecedented times by providing resources to help price products
    and services to in order to mitigate losses.
